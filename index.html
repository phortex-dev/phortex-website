What we build

Phortex AI focuses on practical, high-leverage agentic systems — starting with your existing tools, data, and teams.

Agentic copilots for teams
Custom copilots that understand your documents, tickets, and systems — answering questions, drafting responses, and coordinating tasks across tools.
RAG over private data Knowledge bases Internal chat interfaces
End-to-end automation agents
Multi-step agents that watch events (email, webhooks, logs), make decisions, call APIs, and open or update tickets — with humans looped in where it matters.
n8n · HTTP APIs Incident triage Ops workflows
Evaluation & “agent arenas”
Harnesses that test different models, prompts, and agent graphs on the same scenarios — so you can choose what actually performs best, not just looks smart in a demo.

Early use cases we love
